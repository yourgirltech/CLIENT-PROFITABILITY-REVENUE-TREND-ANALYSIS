# Financial Performance & Client Profitability Analysis

This project analyzes a company's 2015 financial data using Microsoft Excel to evaluate overall financial performance, profitability trends, revenue contribution by client segment, and individual client profitability.

The analysis includes annual revenue and gross profit calculations, monthly performance tracking, revenue mix analysis, gross profit margin evaluation, and client profitability comparisons. Excel functions such as SUMIFS were used to aggregate and analyze data, while charts were created to visualize trends and support business decision-making.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Financial Analysis
* Gross Profit & Margin Analysis
* Revenue Analysis
* Client Profitability Analysis
* SUMIFS
* Data Visualization
* Business Reporting
* Microsoft Excel

## Key Objectives

* Assess annual financial performance
* Analyze monthly revenue and profitability trends
* Evaluate revenue contribution by client segment
* Identify the most profitable clients based on gross profit and gross profit margin
